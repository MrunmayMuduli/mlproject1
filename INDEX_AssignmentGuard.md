## üí° KEY DIFFERENTIATORS

### Why AssignmentGuard is Different
1. **Governance First** - Most AI tools add governance later; we built it in from the start
2. **Transparent Design** - Users understand exactly what AI is doing and why
3. **Explicit Bias Detection** - Rule-based bias detection, not just LLM judgment
4. **Human-Centered** - AI supplements but never replaces instructor judgment
5. **Audit Ready** - Regular testing built into system design from day one
6. **Fair by Default** - Specific protections for ESL, neurodivergent, and diverse learners
